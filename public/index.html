About the Institute

B&R JT Research Co. Limited serves as a cross-border research and knowledge platform on construction contracts and dispute prevention under the Belt and Road context. We connect scholars, practitioners, and institutions through practical, policy-relevant research.

Core Functions

⚖️ Applied Research

Produce comparative legal and policy research for international construction projects.

🤝 Capacity Building

Deliver seminars, forums, and collaborative programs for legal and project professionals.

📚 Public Resources

Maintain open access references, templates, and guidance materials for stakeholders.

🌐 Regional Dialogue

Support cooperation among institutions in Asia and partner jurisdictions.

Welcome Message

Our institute is committed to delivering credible, practical and forward-looking research services for the construction and legal sectors. We welcome collaborations with universities, industry associations, and professional communities seeking evidence-based insights.
